the Legacy of the US Constitution

(In the Context of the 18th Amendment)




First of all, what is legacy?

Legacy is what we are known for, it is our successes and our mistakes, it is why people love us or hate us; legacy is how we will be remembered.
In the context of the 18th amendment, legacy is very much about mistakes.



Below are some images of people. You will remember who they are, because they have a legacy. One or more of these people have a negative legacy, because they did bad things in their lifetime. They still have a legacy, because all of these people are still taught about in schools or are talked about in other places.
